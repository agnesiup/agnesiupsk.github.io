Team canvas

Another long-lasting group project started with making team canvas. We were not able to chose the group ourselves, therefore it was crucial to define the rules and values of a team. I had a chance to be in a group with a completelly different individuals, and it was interesting to watch how everyone of us put efforts in purifying the team canvas. Overall, canvases helped us not only in working proccess, but also in communicating and understanding each other.
