Mobile first

Our very first task was to create a website for mobile phone. Most of the students did not understand completely what the phrase “mobile first” mean. Therefore, pupils paying attention more on a desktop version website; I did that too.

I remember that week as one of the most challenging ones both because of the completely new field at that time – coding – and also because it was a task for an exam.

While carrying out the website, I have gained a lot. First and foremost, I have learned what a HTML, CSS brackets, W3schools and Cyberduck mean. Also I have noticed how important it is to be attentive while coding, because any irrelevant symbol can mess up all the work. Last but not least, I have found out what a tearm blue note is. Glad to say, I enjoyed it a lot and the style inspired me in many ways during the project and afterwards.
