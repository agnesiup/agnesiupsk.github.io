Week submition

During this task, all the students had to illustrate self-created short story and a spritesheet. The dramaturgic curve had to consist of a prelude, point of no return, climax and fade out.

I remember I have enjoyed this task a lot. The only struggle was that it took me a long time to create a story line for everything, but the illustration proccess did not feel like studying or learning at all.

Though I didn’t learn many new things about Adobe Illustrator, I have gained theoretical knowledge – I understood what a narrative curve is and how to draw it.
