Final interactive production

It was the very first time we worked in groups for a long time. The project lasted a few weeks and we had to create an interactive animation based on a movie. We chose “The Shining”. This task had a special focus on the target group who would might be interested in seeing this particular movie.

Each of us had to divide responsibilities and tasks. Our group consisted of 4 girls, and I happened to get the main designer role. Unfortunately, the main group coder appeared in a hospital and during the presentation day all we could present was a prototype of a task. Still, the interactive production task is one of the projects I am proud the most – I put a lot of effort while illustrating and at the end I was satisfied with the result.
